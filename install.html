// install loglinkr as an app

Get the app on your phone.
No app store needed.

Loglinkr is a PWA — installs in seconds, opens like any other app, works offline, sends push notifications. Pick your device below.

  1. Open Chrome on your Android phone.
  2. Go to loglinkr.com/app and sign in.
  3. Tap the three-dot menu (top right).
  4. Tap Install app (or Add to Home screen).
  5. Confirm — Loglinkr appears on your home screen.
  6. Open it from the new icon — full-screen, no browser bar.
💡 Heads-up
  • Works in Chrome, Edge, Brave, Samsung Internet.
  • Already installed but seeing the URL bar? Uninstall the old icon, then reinstall — the manifest moved to /app.
  • Allow notifications when prompted so you don't miss alerts & tasks.
  1. Open Safari on your iPhone or iPad. (Must be Safari — Chrome on iOS can't install PWAs.)
  2. Go to loglinkr.com/app and sign in.
  3. Tap the Share button (square with up-arrow, bottom of screen).
  4. Scroll down and tap Add to Home Screen.
  5. Tap Add — Loglinkr appears on your home screen.
  6. Open it from the new icon — full-screen, no Safari bar.
💡 Heads-up
  • iOS only allows install via Safari, not Chrome / Firefox.
  • Push notifications require iOS 16.4 or newer.
  • Use a Loglinkr account — sign in once and it stays signed in.
  1. Open Chrome or Edge on your laptop / desktop.
  2. Go to loglinkr.com/app and sign in.
  3. Look for the install icon in the address bar (computer with down-arrow, right side).
  4. Click it → click Install. Or use menu → Install Loglinkr…
  5. Loglinkr opens in its own window, with its own taskbar / dock icon.
💡 Heads-up
  • Works in Chrome, Edge, Brave on Windows / macOS / Linux.
  • Safari on Mac doesn't install PWAs — use a Chromium browser.
  • Right-click the dock / taskbar icon to pin it.
